\subsection{SCARE}

The The Sentiment Corpus of App Reviews with Fine-grained Annotations in German \cite{sanger2016scare} is hand-annotated corpus that asserts so called polarities to reviews of apps.
What is interesting about it is, that the annotations are very fine-grained:
Not the whole review gets labelled for a certain polarity --- i.e. \textit{positive}, \textit{negative}, or \textit{neutral} --- but aspects and subjective (sub-phrases).
This means that in a given review, let's say ``guter wecker... || vom prinzip her echt gut...aber grade was die sprachausgabe betrifft noch etwas buggy....'' there are the following annotations:

Three aspects
\begin{itemize}
	\item Wecker --- neutral
	\item Prinzip --- neutral
	\item Sprachausgabe --- neutral
\end{itemize}

Three subjectives
\begin{itemize}
	\item guter --- positive
	\item echt gut --- positive
	\item etwas buggy --- negative
\end{itemize}
